About Us

We work on Tree plantation and biodiversity conservation

Bengal Tree Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to restoring and protecting the natural world by focusing on the essential relationship between trees and bird populations. Our mission is to combat climate change, preserve biodiversity, and create sustainable ecosystems through large-scale tree planting and biodiversity conservation initiatives.

our mission is to create a greener, more sustainable future while empowering rural children with education and life skills. Through large-scale tree plantation drives, we combat climate change, restore biodiversity, and promote environmental awareness. Simultaneously, we work towards the holistic development of underprivileged children in rural areas by providing quality education, skill training, and essential resources. By blending ecological responsibility with social upliftment, we aim to build self-reliant communities and a healthier planet for future generations. Together, we can grow a better tomorrow—one tree, one child, one village at a time.

Learn more at Our Projects

we envision a future where every child in rural India has access to quality education and every community thrives in a green, sustainable environment. We strive to create a world where environmental conservation and social development go hand in hand—where lush forests purify our air, enrich our soil, and provide a healthier planet for future generations. Through tree plantations and rural development initiatives, we aim to empower communities with the knowledge, resources, and opportunities needed to break the cycle of poverty while fostering a deep-rooted connection with nature. Our vision is to nurture both young minds and the earth, ensuring a brighter, greener, and more equitable future for all.

How NGOs Are Making a Difference

NGOs worldwide are implementing innovative and impactful programs to promote tree plantation and environmental awareness. Here are a few organizations making a difference:

  • Green Belt Movement: Founded by Wangari Maathai, this organization empowers communities, especially women, to plant trees and combat deforestation in Africa.
  • Trees for the Future: This NGO focuses on agroforestry projects that improve food security and livelihoods for farmers in developing countries.
  • Eden Reforestation Projects: A leader in large-scale reforestation, Eden works to restore ecosystems while creating jobs in impoverished areas.
  • One Tree Planted: Operating globally, this organization simplifies the tree-planting process by allowing individuals and businesses to fund projects in various regions.
  • Arbor Day Foundation: A pioneer in tree conservation and education, the foundation promotes reforestation efforts across North America.
  • Grow Billion Tree: Empowering individuals and organizations to take action on air pollution and climate change through tree planting and conservation efforts.
  • SankalpTaru: SankalpTaru Foundation is an IT-enabled, environmentally-focused NGO.
  • Say Trees: To make citizens aware about the need of trees for our survival and protect natural resources for generations to come
  • Tree Kishan: Revolutionizing the farm-grown wood market, TreeKisanTM is an AI- based Platform to provide the best value to tree farmers.
  • Van Mahotsav: A Commitment to Green India: Bridging Government Initiatives and Community Participation for a Greener Future
  • Down to Earth: India was a tree planting laboratory for 200 years.

Our Mission at Bengal Tree Foundation

At Bengal Tree Foundation, we are dedicated to planting trees and restoring degraded lands. Our mission focuses on:

  • Reforestation in deforested areas.
  • Promoting community participation in tree plantation drives.
  • Educating youth about the importance of trees and climate action.

We collaborate with global organizations and local communities to create lasting change. Partner with us to make a difference—visit https://treefoundationbengal.web.app today!

Join the Movement: Transforming the Planet Through Tree Plantation 🌱

Tree plantation is more than just a solution to environmental issues—it’s a powerful act of hope and renewal. With deforestation, climate change, and urban sprawl threatening our planet, planting trees has become a critical mission for ensuring a sustainable future. NGOs worldwide are at the forefront of this movement, driving innovative projects to protect and restore ecosystems. Let’s explore the impact of tree plantations and how you can support this vital cause.

Why Tree Plantation Matters

  1. Mitigating Climate Change: Trees absorb carbon dioxide, reducing greenhouse gas concentrations in the atmosphere.
  2. Purifying Air: They filter pollutants and release life-giving oxygen.
  3. Supporting Biodiversity: Forests provide habitats for 80% of terrestrial species, from birds to insects.
  4. Preventing Soil Erosion: Trees stabilize soil and reduce the impact of floods and landslides.
  5. Improving Livelihoods: Community-based forestry supports sustainable income for local populations.
