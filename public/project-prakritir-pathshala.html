Prakritir Pathshala - A Unique Initiative by Bengal Tree Foundation

About Us

Bengal Tree Foundation is committed to creating a better future by nurturing nature and supporting underprivileged children. Through the Prakritir Pathshala project, we not only focus on tree plantation but also provide essential food items and basic educational materials to poor school students, improving their quality of life.

Prakritir Pathshala Project

Prakritir Pathshala is a humanitarian initiative by Bengal Tree Foundation aimed at creating a healthier and more educational environment for underprivileged children. Through this project, we:

  • Plant and Preserve Trees: Implement tree plantation programs in schools to promote a greener environment.

  • Provide Food Assistance: Supply nutritious meals to children suffering from malnutrition.

  • Distribute Educational Materials: Provide books, notebooks, pens, pencils, and other essential study materials to underprivileged students.

  • Promote Environmental Awareness: Educate children on the importance of protecting nature and planting trees.

Why Is This Project Important?

Many children in India lack access to proper education and healthcare due to poverty. Malnutrition hampers their physical and mental development. At the same time, climate change is disrupting the ecological balance. To address these challenges, we launched the Prakritir Pathshala initiative.

Our Activities

  1. School-Based Tree Plantation Programs: Planting fruit-bearing, medicinal, and shade-providing trees within school premises to create a sustainable environment.

  2. Nutritional Meal Distribution: Providing monthly healthy meals to schoolchildren to ensure their well-being.

  3. Environmental Education Workshops: Raising awareness among children about the significance of trees and environmental conservation.

  4. Essential Study Material Support: Helping underprivileged children continue their education by supplying necessary learning resources.

Our Goals

  • To cultivate environmental consciousness among future generations.

  • To ensure the mental and physical well-being of poor children.

  • To increase greenery within school environments.

  • To build an aware society committed to environmental conservation.

  • Become a Volunteer: Join our initiatives and contribute to shaping a brighter future for children.

  • Spread Awareness: Share information about Prakritir Pathshala with your friends and family to encourage environmental consciousness.
